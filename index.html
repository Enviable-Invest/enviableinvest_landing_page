FAQs

How do I start receiving my Drivers Income Payout through EnviableInvest?

To begin, download the EnviableInvest app from the Google Play Store and complete the sign-up process.

Once registered, engage with our transportation division either online or by visiting one of our offices to purchase your desired automobile asset.

After completing this process, your Drivers Income Payout will be available for seamless withdrawal through the app.

What is Drivers Income Payout (DIP)?

Drivers Income Payout (DIP) is the income generated from transportation services provided by assets such as buses, tricycles, and cars operated by Enviable Transport Ltd. This income is available to you through the EnviableInvest app for easy access.

Is EnviableInvest an investment platform?

No, EnviableInvest is designed solely for accessing and withdrawing your Drivers Income Payout, making it convenient for you to receive earnings from assets managed by Enviable Transport Ltd without waiting for manual bank payments.

How do I withdraw my Drivers Income Payout?

Once your DIP is credited to your wallet, you can use the app to withdraw funds directly into your local bank account, ensuring a smooth and prompt transfer.

Why is KYC required, and how does it work?

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required to validate your identity and ensure compliance with regulatory standards.

Simply upload your KYC documents through the app after signing up to gain full access to the DIP withdrawal feature.

What security measures does EnviableInvest have in place to protect my data?

EnviableInvest employs strong security measures, including encryption and secure access controls, to keep your personal and financial information safe.

How frequently will I receive my Drivers Income Payout?

DIP earnings are generated based on the operational schedule of the assets managed by Enviable Transport Ltd. The app will notify you when payouts are available for withdrawal, allowing you to access your funds conveniently.

Are there any charges for using EnviableInvest or withdrawing my Drivers Income Payout?

There are no fees for using the app or withdrawing your funds. However, your bank may apply standard transaction fees for transfers.

What should I do if I have questions or need assistance with the app?

For support, please use the in-app support feature or contact our customer service team for help with any inquiries.

Do I need to accept any terms and conditions?

Yes, before completing your KYC verification, you’ll be prompted to review and accept the terms and conditions, which outline EnviableInvest’s services and usage guidelines.
